Older versions of the Regulations

Until 2011, the Regulations were maintained by Ron van Bruchem and the WCA Board. Since then, the WCA Regulations Committee is in charge of them.
For the 2013 release, the Regulations were split into two documents: the Regulations and the Guidelines.
